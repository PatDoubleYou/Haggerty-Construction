A modern home office demands more than basic shelving. We designed and built this custom floor-to-ceiling bookcase to emphasize the lofty height of the room, bringing an air of serious luxury and solid craftsmanship to the workspace.

Every shelf is precisely leveled, every joint is tight, and the finish was hand-applied across multiple coats to achieve a depth that catches the light just right. This is the kind of piece that makes a room.
