A full kitchen transformation centered around a massive custom knotty alder island. We pulled the entire space down to the studs, reworking the layout to accommodate modern appliances and vastly improved storage.

Every detail—from the grain selection on the alder to the soft-close hardware—was chosen to balance rustic warmth with modern functionality. The result is a kitchen that's as beautiful to look at as it is practical to cook in.
