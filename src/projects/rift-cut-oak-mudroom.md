Beautiful doesn't mean fragile. We designed these rift-cut white oak mudroom cabinets to handle daily abuse while looking like a high-end furniture piece. Built-in storage hooks, a concealed broom closet, and deep cubbies ensure everything has its place.

The rift-cut technique gives the oak a consistent, straight grain pattern that resists warping and looks incredibly clean. It's one of those details that separates custom work from off-the-shelf solutions.
