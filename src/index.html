Built by Family. Backed by Legacy.

Haggerty Construction is a St. George family business with serious roots in the trade. Our grandfather, Patrick R. Haggerty, managed construction for the U.S. Antarctic Program — they even named Haggerty Hill after him.

Our founder, William, served 20+ years in the National Guard and cut his teeth at Baker Concrete, working on massive projects like the Cincinnati Reds Stadium. Today, the team is tighter: William leads the vision, Blake runs Project Management, and Jayden builds the custom software that keeps our jobs running like clockwork.

We take on high-end residential projects that matter to the people who live in them. If you want a builder who values communication, details, and doing it right the first time — we're your guys.
